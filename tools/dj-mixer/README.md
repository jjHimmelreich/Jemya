# DJ Mixer

A DJ mixing tool that lets you blend, cue, and mix your playlists like a real DJ deck.

> **Standalone app — lives in its own repository:**
> 👉 [github.com/jjHimmelreich/DJMixer](https://github.com/jjHimmelreich/DJMixer)

## What it does

DJ Mixer brings DJ-deck controls to your music playlists — load two tracks side by side, control playback independently, cross-fade between them, and build live mixes on the fly.
